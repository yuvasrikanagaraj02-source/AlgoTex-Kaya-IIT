# 🏗️ Project Overview

**BuildWise AI 2.0** is an AI-powered Autonomous Construction Supply Chain Operating System designed to help construction companies prevent project delays before they occur.

Instead of simply monitoring construction activities, BuildWise AI continuously analyzes the supply chain, predicts disruptions, recommends the best actions, and assists managers in making faster and smarter decisions.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twins, Multi-Agent Systems, and Predictive Analytics into one enterprise solution.

---

# 🚧 Problem Statement

Construction projects often experience delays because of:

- Late material deliveries
- Poor inventory visibility
- Inefficient supplier management
- Manual procurement processes
- Lack of real-time monitoring
- Weather and traffic disruptions
- Unexpected external risks

Most existing systems only report problems after they occur, making it difficult to take proactive actions.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

BuildWise AI 2.0 helps construction companies prevent delays before they happen.

The platform uses:

- Digital Twin
- Multi-Agent AI
- Real-Time Risk Intelligence
- What-If Simulation
- AI Procurement Optimization

to continuously monitor projects, predict risks, recommend the best decisions, and optimize the construction supply chain.
